WHO

WE

ARE

Lifesaver Mission Bangladesh is a non-profit organization based out of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Our Vision:

We desire as a Christian Community, to provide a Christ centred organization in Bangladesh. Our mission is to serve and minister to the needy and poor people, mostly the children and women in the Bangladesh provinces.

Our Long Term Goals:

It is the desire of the Lifesaver Mission Bangladesh as an organization, to work in cooperation with others who share our aims and objectives of helping others, and to gain the support of both individuals and organization to achieve Matthew 25: 34-40.

As an organization, the long term aims are twofold: Firstly, to help distraught people in Bangladesh. Secondly, to demonstrate our Christian faith by practical Christian service to the ones in most dire need.

We seek to accomplish these objectives in the following ways:

  1. By supporting and sponsoring the needy by providing, whenever and wherever possible, relief and support to those in most need. By supplying food, clothing and blankets and other essential supplies.

  2. Education and Vocational Training for being self dependent. By promoting the building and establishing of schools, medical clinics and mobile medication campaign.

  3. By supplying and distributing food, cloths for suffering people which are necessary in disaster and crisis time.

  4. By practical service in the area of social responsibility and development.

Sponsorship Project

Project Area:

Sirajgonj, Bhuiagati, Nimgachi, Bangladesh.

Project Duration:

August 2014 to August 2019. Five Year’s Support Plan.

Objective:

We are helping 30 school children of Class 3, Class 4 and Class 5 for five years. They will be provided all their educational needs with some extra needs like cloths, toys, sports things and awareness building leaflet, posters and books for their families. We are trying to help their families to be self-dependent and help to create income generating schemes.

Children to be Helped:

30 school children from an ethnic community named (MAHATO) will be directly involved with this support scheme. Furthermore their families are included in the long term development program.

Action Plan for Children:

  • One of our representatives will be continually there for observing the progress and taking different actions directed by the directors.

  • One pastor and his wife will be involved to communicate the children’s families.

  • The country director will visit 30 families and children once a month to see the progress and direct the actions to be taken all around the month.

  • Each children will be provided half of their school fees, necessary books, papers, pens, pencils and everything related to their education.

  • One expert teacher will go with director to teach the children and stay there for two days and teach them the update educational system.

  • One college student will be continually there to do tuition for children 4 days a week. He will be supported a fixed allowance for his voluntary work for children, and this support will directly go for his own college expenses and educational needs.

Free Mobile Medication

Objective:

First one is to share GOD by serving people. Lifesaver Mission Bangladesh conducts monthly Free Mobile Medical Camp for women and children in far rural areas where people can not reach a doctor or get medicine in crisis time. Our medical team and volunteers go to different areas and provide free medication and medicine. Love GOD love the people.

Action Area:

Bangladesh a south Asian country. Gopalgonj is one of 64 districts of Bangladesh. It is a low land area named ‘Bilchanda’ which is one of the biggest lowland in Bangladesh. 40 villages are present in this lowland. In every rainy season all the 40 villages flooded with water. In this time the whole area get disconnected from the main land. The only way to communicate left then the boat. Every family don’t have that. So they have to suffer a lot to get these things in time. Financially they are depended on agriculture. Hundreds of women die in the time of their child’s birth. They don’t get proper medicine when they are in need. Some specific disease like diarrhea, skin diseases, dysentery, fever and malnutrition attack people in the rainy season as the accommodation becomes wet. They have the food scarcity in this time. People fast for two meals a day sometime. They get just lunch one day.

Program Plan:

In June, July, August, September and October we go for medical camp basically. But we go for the camp in crisis time if necessary in special need. Our free mobile medical camp service is available for the local people every day of these three and half month. We provide free medicine and treatment to the needy people of far rural areas of Bangladesh. People come from different villages by boat every day from dawn to dusk.

Disaster time Support

Lifesaver Mission Bangladesh is always beside the endangered people. People in Bangladesh face and survive fighting against frequent natural disaster.

We always stand beside the people affected by natural calamities. We support basically marginal people in rural areas of Bangladesh.

We conduct flood relief camps, warm cloths for cold victims in winter, pure drinking water supply in emergency basis, and we try to be stand beside the people in climate change prone areas of Bangladesh.
